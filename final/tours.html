Join the Adventure in Cozumel With GoScoots

Cozumel, a mostly undeveloped Mexican island in the Caribbean Sea, is a popular cruise ship port of call famed for its scuba diving The Cozumel Port gets millions of cruise ship passengers each year. The Port of Cozumel was rated #3 for the best cruise ports in the World. The Island was also rated as one of the top safest places in the World to live or travel.
